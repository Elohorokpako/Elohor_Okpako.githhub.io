About Me

I am a Microbiology graduate with a knack for self development and constant evolvment I am currently pursuing my Masters in Infectious Diseases and One Health through the Erasmus Mundus Scholarship. My current Master Thesis is based on developing a pipeline for the analysis of Whole Genome Sequences from Mycobacterium tuberculosis species.
