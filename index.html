About Me

As a Microbiology graduate, I possess a passion for self-development and continuous evolution. Currently, I am pursuing my Masters in Infectious Diseases and One Health through the Erasmus Mundus Scholarship program. My current thesis focuses onn developing a pipeline for the analysis of Whole Genome Sequences from Mycobacterium tuberculosis species for my Master's thesis. My interests span across Machine Learning, Bioinformatics, and Computational Biology, as I am driven to utilize these disciplines to solve complex biological problems.
